# purpose.md

## Vault Mission

This vault is designed to become a long-term knowledge operating system for Obsidian and AI agents.

Its goal is to connect raw sources, research, structured wiki knowledge, projects, daily work, content creation, and decision records into one traceable and reusable system.

## What This Vault Is For

Use this vault to manage:

- external sources and evidence
- deep research and technical analysis
- structured concepts, entities, workflows, claims, and synthesis
- project planning and execution
- daily planning and review
- high-value AI conversations
- content creation assets and drafts
- technical, architecture, product, content, and roadmap decisions

## What This Vault Is Not For

This vault is not meant to become:

- a random file dump
- a place for unsourced AI summaries
- a purely task-management system
- a purely writing workspace
- an automatic RAG product
- a replacement for Obsidian

## Core Principles

1. Raw sources are evidence and should remain traceable.
2. Research is analysis, not final knowledge by itself.
3. Wiki pages should be reusable and evolving.
4. Important decisions should be auditable.
5. Project experience should feed the knowledge base.
6. Content creation should grow from existing knowledge.
7. Old conclusions should be reviewed when they become stale.

## Primary Knowledge Flow

```text
Raw Sources → Research → Wiki Integration → Decisions / Projects / Content → Review
```

## Success Criteria

This vault is working well when:

- important conclusions can be traced to sources
- useful conversations are not lost in chat history
- research produces reusable knowledge pages
- projects produce reusable lessons
- content drafts reuse existing research and knowledge
- decisions can be reviewed later with their original context
